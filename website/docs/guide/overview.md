# Milki Launcher Overview

Milki is a free and open-source, privacy-respecting Android launcher focused on search-driven usage. Milki places a powerful [search dialog](./search-dialog) at the center of your experience.
Milki is designed to work perfectly out-of-the-box, but gives you the freedom to tweak almost every aspect of the UI and behavior.

- **Privacy First:** We don't track your app usage, collect telemetry, or show ads. Your data stays on your device.
- **Search-Driven:** Find exactly what you need—apps, contacts, web results, or settings—instantly.
- **Free & Open:** Free as in freedom and free of charge—nothing is ever gated behind a paywall.
- **Open Ecosystem:** 100% open source, welcoming community contributions.
